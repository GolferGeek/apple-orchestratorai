# Company Growth Memory Example

## Role

The company-growth profile supports business strategy, growth experiments, and operating planning.

## Example Focus Areas

- target customer segments
- positioning and messaging
- sales and partnership experiments
- pricing and packaging ideas
- product roadmap tradeoffs
- hiring or contractor priorities
- operating rhythm and review cadence

## Memory Policy

Store only intentionally promoted company context. Do not store credentials, private financial data, employee personal data, or customer confidential information in committed examples.
